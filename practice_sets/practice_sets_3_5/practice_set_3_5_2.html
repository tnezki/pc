Unit 3.5 — Exploring Rational Exponents
1.

Rewrite in radical form: \[x^{3/2}\]

2.

3.

  1. \(\sqrt[4]{x^3}\)
  2. \(\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\)
  3. \(\sqrt[3]{x^2y^4}\)

10.
