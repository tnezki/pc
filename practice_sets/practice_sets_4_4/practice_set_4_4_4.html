Unit 4.4 — Graphing and Writing Trig Functions
1.

State whether the following functions have the same amplitude, same period, both, or neither.

  1. \(y=\sin(x)\) and \(y=2\sin(x)\)
  2. \(y=\sin(x)\) and \(y=\sin(2x)\)
  3. \(y=3\cos(4x)\) and \(y=-3\sin(4x)\)
